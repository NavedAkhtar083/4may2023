Anxious dog? Here's how diet can help manage their mental health

On the occasion of Dog Anxiety Awareness Week, Dr. Deepak Saraswat, Head Veterinarian, Zigly, shares with HT Lifestyle, some ways to incorporate diet into managing your dog's anxiety.

Protein is an essential nutrient for dogs and is critical for maintaining healthy muscles and tissues. But did you know that protein can also affect your dog's mood? Studies have shown that diets high in protein can increase serotonin levels in the brain, which is a natural mood stabilizer. SCO foreign ministers meet to boost cooperation in security, trade

Foreign ministers of the Shanghai Cooperation Organisation (SCO) will meet on Friday to prepare the agenda for the group's summit in July, where 15 decisions or proposals will be signed off. The measures will boost

cooperation in trade, commerce, security and socio-cultural affairs among SCO members. The meeting is expected to discuss global issues such as the Ukraine crisis and the situation in Afghanistan, but India-Pakistan relations will not be discussed. The SCO was formed in 2001 and comprises China, Kazakhstan, Kyrgyzstan, Russia, Tajikistan, Uzbekistan, India and Pakistan, with Belarus, Iran and Mongolia as observers.
